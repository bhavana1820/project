Here are some current job openings in Deloitte's Technology Consultancy division:

  1. Cloud Engineering Consultant

    As a Cloud Engineering Consultant, you will work with clients to solve business problems using cloud technologies. Your role will involve bringing together industry insights, business domain knowledge, and cloud engineering expertise to help clients innovate faster and enhance their agility. You'll participate in the development of new products and services and help clients run their businesses more efficiently. This position requires strong analytical skills, experience with cloud platforms like AWS or Azure, and a solid understanding of business operations​

  2. Analytics and Cognitive Specialist

    This role involves working with data to uncover insights and drive decision-making for clients. You will leverage technologies like AI, machine learning, and robotics to analyze large datasets and create actionable intelligence. Responsibilities include developing and managing data models, creating predictive analytics solutions, and collaborating with clients to understand their business needs and implement data-driven strategies. A background in data science, statistics, or a related field is essential for this role​

  3. Technology Strategy and Transformation Advisor

    As a Technology Strategy and Transformation Advisor, you will help IT leaders navigate the complexities of modern technology landscapes. This role involves providing strategic advice on technology adoption, digital transformation, and IT operational improvements. You will work closely with senior executives to align technology initiatives with business goals, manage transformation projects, and ensure that technology investments deliver maximum value. Experience in IT consulting, project management, and strategic planning is required

  4. Enterprise Technology and SAP Consultant

    In this position, you will guide businesses through the implementation lifecycle of enterprise systems, particularly SAP solutions. Responsibilities include strategy setting, system design, deployment, and optimization. You will work on enterprise-wide systems and solutions for specific organizational functions such as IT, finance, and supply chain. Knowledge of SAP software and experience in enterprise technology implementations are key for this role

  5. Customer & Marketing Technology Specialist

    This role focuses on helping clients leverage digital technologies to enhance their customer engagement and marketing strategies. You will work on digital strategy, mobile, social/web, content management, and managed services projects. Your goal will be to align clients' business aspirations with user goals, using innovative technologies to drive online and mobile engagement. A background in digital marketing, UX/UI design, and technology implementation is ideal for this position

Application Instructions

Common Components of Application Instructions

    1.Method of Submission:


    • Email: Applicants are often asked to email their resume and cover letter to a specific email address. Example: "Please email your resume and cover letter to careers@deloitte.com."

    • Online Application Portal: Some companies use an online system where candidates must upload their documents. Example: "Submit your application through our careers portal at www.deloitte.com/careers."

    • Mail: Though less common today, some organizations might request physical copies. Example: "Mail your resume and cover letter to Deloitte, 123 Main St, Anytown, USA."


    2.Required Documents:


    • Resume: A summary of your work experience, education, skills, and achievements.

    • Cover Letter: A personalized letter explaining why you are a good fit for the position.

    • References: A list of professional references or recommendation letters.

    • Portfolio: For creative or technical positions, a portfolio of work samples.

    3.Format and File Type:


    • Companies often specify the format in which documents should be submitted (PDF, DOCX, etc.). Example: "Submit your resume and cover letter in PDF format."

    4.Subject Line for Emails:


    • Specific instructions on what to include in the subject line can help ensure your application is processed correctly. Example: "Subject Line: Application for Financial Analyst Position – [Your Name]"

    5.Deadline:

    • The final date by which applications must be submitted. Example: "Applications must be received by June 30, 2024."

    6.Additional Information:

    • Any other requirements such as answering specific questions, providing a writing sample, or completing an application form.

  4. Application Deadline:

    • Ensure that your application is submitted before the deadline.

  5. Additional Requirements:

    • Answer the following question in your cover letter: "What experience do you have with financial analysis tools and software?"

Tips for Following Application Instructions

  • Read Carefully: Thoroughly read the instructions to ensure you understand all requirements.

  • Double-Check Documents: Make sure your documents are correctly formatted and named as specified.

  • Follow Up: If you do not receive a confirmation within a week, consider following up to ensure your application was received.

  • Professionalism: Use a professional tone and email address when corresponding with potential employers.
